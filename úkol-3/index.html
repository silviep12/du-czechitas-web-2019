Briefly About Me

My name is Silvie Průchová.

I live in České Budějovice. I graduated from South Bohemian University and Charles University in Prague. I am qualified both in the field of Science and Languages. However, no university is able to prepare you for the ups and downs of parentig, which has been my biggest challenge so far.

Whenever it's possible we travel as a family. Travelling abroad helps my children to look at the life from a different perspective and last but not least helps them understand why learning foreign languages is so important.

Travelling With The Kids

Travelling with the kids is not always so easy. Everything is about good planning and timing. The successful holiday means they mustn't get bored and must be kept entertained all the time. Ideally you mix physical activity with getting to know new things. Thankfully one doesn't have to travel so far to get all this. Austria is a real paradise for little explorers like this Triassic Park. Enjoy your trip back in time!
